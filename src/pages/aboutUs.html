Who We Are

Bellamy & Associates is a holistic therapy, coaching, and energy healing practice and we believe healing is personal, but it’s also political, cultural, and collective.

We’re committed to supporting people in healing not just their individual wounds, but also the impacts of systems, identities, and lived experiences that shape their stories.

We created this resource hub to support folks who are navigating their own growth while also reckoning with the world around them.

This space brings together resources on mental health, intersectionality, disability justice, body liberation, and more. It's a reflection of how we work - with compassion, nuance, and deep respect for each person's lived experience.

Our team brings diverse lived experiences and clinical training to the work we do. We specialize in somatic therapy, dance/movement therapy, trauma healing, OCD support, couples work, and coaching for folks healing from trauma, anxiety, and depression and navigating identity, burnout, and life transitions.

If what you find here resonates, we’d love to support you more directly through our therapy, coaching, or energy healing offerings. You deserve care that honors the whole of who you are.
