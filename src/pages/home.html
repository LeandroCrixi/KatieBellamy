A space to gather, share, and grow - for individual healing and collective liberation.

What began as a simple resource list for our holistic therapy, coaching, and energy healing practice has evolved into a growing, dynamic hub for learning, reflection, and connection.

Here, you'll find curated tools, articles, and links that reflect our values: mental health access, body liberation, disability justice, queer inclusion, intersectionality, and collective care.

We created this space not just to support our clients, but to contribute to broader conversations about wellness, equity, and healing.

Special thanks to Ashantis Jones, whose vision, time, and expertise helped bring this project to life. Their contributions were foundational in shaping this as a space that honors both lived experience and professional insight.

We hope you find something here that affirms, inspires, or supports you - and we invite you to return often, share widely, and be part of this evolving conversation.
